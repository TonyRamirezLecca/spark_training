About Spark Training

Spark Training was made from one simple idea. One simple idea, turned into another, and soon there was a fire burning to change the fitness industry. Spark Training is for those who no longer want to waste their time and money by buying gimmicky and false fads of diets, workouts, and any other “magic pills” from a growing industry. Through working online with one-on-one clientele or in small groups, we deliver what you want. This includes teaching you about the things that matter: nutrition and exercise, not just exercise alone. You set the goal and we help you accomplish it while helping you understand how to maintain a new, healthier lifestyle. At Spark Training, we do one thing: turn even one spark into a fire.

One spark can start a fire, and one goal can change a life. So, what’s your spark?
